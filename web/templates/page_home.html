Welcome to Biomarkerz

 

Biomarkers are measurable biological parameters that are predictive of the risk of a serious disease when present at an abnormal level in the human body.  Examples include Blood Pressure,  blood and urine chemistry, imaging.

 

A health claim on food must show (most weight given to human trials)

       * A consistent association between the food or property of the food and the health effect.

       * The effect must be biologically plausible and

       * The amount of food needed to achieve the health effect must be able to be consumed as part of a normal diet.
